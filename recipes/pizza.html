How to make Pizza

If you like ordering pizza from your local pizzeria, you'll love making it at home. No pizza will ever taste as fresh as it does when you eat it straight from your own oven.

This article gives instructions on how to make hot, fresh pizza the fast way, or from scratch. Scroll to the bottom for a section on popular toppings combinations.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • Yeast
  • Pizza sauce
  • Favourite pizza toppings
  • Grated mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Place the dough on a baking tray
  3. Put sauce on the top
  4. Put cheese
  5. Place the toppings
  6. Cook for 15 mins
